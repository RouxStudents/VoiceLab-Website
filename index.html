Roux Students

Google Developer Students Clubs organize peer-to-peer learning initiatives, discussions and volunteer projects for students of all knowledge levels interested in applying computer science to make a positive impact in the world


The Voice and Conversational AI Lab is an open source initiative launched by Roux Students, a Google Developer Student Club started by students from Northastern University's new Roux Institute in Portland, Maine.

The Lab collaborates with students across the GDSC network, private sector companies and nonprofits on carrying out voice and conversational AI research important to the advancement of humanitarian AI applications.
